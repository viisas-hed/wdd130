Paraguay

Paraguay is a country full of friendly people, rich in culture and with a low cost of life (That's great for visitors!). It's located in the middle of South America and surrounded by Bolivia, Argentina and Brasil. The official languages are Spanish and Guarani, a native indigenous language that's spoken or understood by more than 80% of paraguayans
